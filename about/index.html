About

An independent certification body for sound recordings.

The Musical Form Institute publishes standards for how recordings are made, reviews submissions independently against those standards, and maintains a public record of certified work.

Mission

Make how a recording was produced verifiable on the public record.

Anyone can claim their music is human-made. No platform's internal score can verify it across the industry. The Musical Form Institute exists to fill that gap.

We publish two open standards. Each submission is reviewed by trained analysts under those standards. Certified records get a unique ID and appear in the public registry, accessible to anyone for verification. Decisions are appealable. The standards evolve openly.

The Institute is independent of any platform, distributor, label, or rights organization.
