What to expect

Who responds, and how fast.

Email is the fastest path. We aim to respond within one business day. If a question requires deeper review (a methodology question, a contested certification, a press inquiry), the first reply will tell you when to expect a complete response.

  • General questionsEmail certifications@musicalform.org. Common questions are usually covered in the FAQ.
  • Label portal accessUse the same email with subject "Label Portal Access Request." Include your label name and approximate catalog size. See For Labels for context.
  • Press & mediaEmail with subject "Press." Include outlet, deadline, and the angle. We respond same day for time-sensitive requests.
  • Reconsideration of a CEP decisionReconsiderations are submitted in writing within 30 days of the decision. Include the CEP ID and the basis for reconsideration.
  • Standards questionsThe published standards (CSF v2.0, CEP v1.2) are the canonical answer to methodology questions. If something is unclear, email and we will clarify.
