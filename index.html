Process

How certification works

Three steps from submission to public record.

01 · SUBMIT

Submit a recording

Send a YouTube Music playlist link for CSF, or upload a master audio file for CEP. Currently free.

02 · REVIEW

Independent analyst review

Trained analysts assess the recording against the published standard. CSF combines AI detection with human review on edge cases. Every CEP submission is individually reviewed.

03 · CERTIFIED

Public record

Certified records receive a unique ID and appear in the public registry, visible and verifiable to anyone. For CEP submissions, the master audio file is deleted after review and only a SHA-256 hash is retained.
