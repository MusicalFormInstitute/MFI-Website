Process

How certification works.

Three steps from submission to public record.

01 · SUBMIT

Submit a recording.

Send a YouTube Music playlist link for CSF, or upload a master audio file for CEP. Free during introductory period.

02 · REVIEW

Independent analyst review.

Trained analysts assess the recording against the published standard. CSF combines AI detection with human review on edge cases. Every CEP submission is individually reviewed.

03 · CERTIFIED

Public record.

Certified records get a unique ID and appear in the public registry, visible and verifiable to anyone. The audio file is deleted after review; only a SHA-256 hash is retained.

About

Operated by Muse Foundry LLC.

The Musical Form Institute is an independent certification body, founded and directed by Jeffrey Anthony. The mission: make how a recording was produced verifiable on the public record, independent of any platform or distributor.

Our published standards are the basis for every certification. Reviews are conducted by trained analysts. Decisions are appealable. The registry is the public record.
