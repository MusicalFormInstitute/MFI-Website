Why MFI

What labels get from the certification

A short list of what the marks actually do for your release strategy and for your artists.

A platform-independent credential

The mark is yours, not a streaming service's internal score. It travels with the recording across DSPs, sync deals, sync libraries, and press.

Public verifiability

Anyone can look up a CSF or CEP ID in the public registry. Sync agencies, journalists, and listeners can confirm the certification themselves.

Defense against false-positive AI flags

If a DSP's automated tooling flags a recording as AI-generated, having an independent CSF certification on file is a documented counter-record.

Production methodology on the record

The CEP certifies the deeper claim: that the recording was actually performed, not constructed. For artists whose craft is the story, that documentation matters.
