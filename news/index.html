How updates are published

All meaningful updates are posted on this page. Major standards revisions are also republished as new versioned PDFs on the Standards page with a public version-history record.

  • Standards revisionsNew version PDF, dated, with summary on this page and a row in the Standards version-history table.
  • Conference talksPosted here when accepted. Materials linked when available.
  • Registry milestonesNotable thresholds (record counts, label counts, partner additions) noted here.
  • Operational noticesAnything that changes how the Institute operates (review timing, submission policy, fee changes) is announced here in advance of taking effect.
