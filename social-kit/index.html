Caption

Copy caption
Certification applies to the recording only. These graphics announce the certification of a specific sound recording. They do not certify an artist, a label, or a catalog. The public registry at registry.musicalform.org is the authoritative record of active certifications.

How it works

Every certified recording has a unique certification ID listed in the public registry. This page reads the registry and composes the graphics in your browser. Nothing is uploaded and nothing is stored.

Recordings certified ahead of release enter the public registry on their release date. If a newly certified recording does not appear yet, that is usually why.

These graphics are announcements for social feeds. They are not the certification badge. Badge files for packaging, liner notes, and cover art are provided separately with certification. Questions: certifications@musicalform.org.
