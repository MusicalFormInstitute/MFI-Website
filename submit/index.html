Choose your certification

Two marks. Both currently free. Pick the one that matches what you want verified about your recording.

No Generative AI

Certified Significant Form

CSF Standard v2.0 · Currently free

Verifies that your recording was produced without generative AI. Best for releases where AI-generated content is the only thing you need to rule out.

  • YouTube Music playlist link, Spotify, or Deezer URL accepted
  • Typically reviewed within 24 hours
  • AI detection scoring with analyst review on edge cases
  • Whole albums or EPs in a single submission
  • Certified records appear in the public CSF registry
Submit for CSF → Process Certification

Certificate of Embodied Production

CEP Standard v1.2 · Currently free

Verifies that your recording was produced without grid correction, pitch correction, or generative AI. Best for releases where the production methodology is the story.

  • Master audio file required (WAV, FLAC, AIFF, or high-bitrate MP3)
  • Individual analyst review under the published CEP standard
  • Reviewed within 90 business days, often faster
  • Reconsideration available within 30 days of decision
  • Certified records appear in the public CEP registry

Side by Side

How they compare

Both marks live in the public registry. Both are reviewed under published standards. The differences are what each one verifies and what evidence we need.

CSF CEP
What it verifies No generative AI in the recording. No grid correction, no pitch correction, no generative AI. Foundational tracks recorded simultaneously.
What you submit A YouTube Music playlist link (preferred), Spotify, or Deezer URL. The master audio file (WAV, FLAC, AIFF, or high-bitrate MP3).
Review process Automated AI detection with documented scoring. Analyst review on indeterminate scores. Individual analyst review against the published checklist for every submission.
Typical timeline Under 24 hours. Up to 90 business days, often much faster.
Cost Currently free. Currently free.
Bulk submissions Single albums or EPs supported through the form. Catalog uploads via label portal. One recording at a time through the form. Catalog uploads via label portal.
If rejected Written decision provided. Resubmit after addressing the finding. Written decision provided. Reconsideration available within 30 days.
