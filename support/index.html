Support the Institute
You choose the amount. Payment is processed by Stripe.

Support does not buy certification. A recording meets the standard or it does not. No payment changes a decision, advances a queue, or creates any standing with the Institute. Certification applies to individual recordings, never to an artist, a label, or a catalog, and that does not change for anyone.

Not a charitable donation. The Musical Form Institute is operated by Muse Foundry LLC, which is not a 501(c)(3) organization. Support is not tax deductible. It is not a purchase, and it carries no goods, services, or benefits in return.

Questions are welcome through the contact page.
