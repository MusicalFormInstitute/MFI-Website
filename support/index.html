The Institute

Support the Institute

Certification is free. The public registry is free to search. What sits behind them is not.

The Musical Form Institute certifies sound recordings and publishes every certification in a public registry that anyone can search, without an account and without a fee.

The work behind that is ongoing. Audio analysis runs per recording. Storage, hosting, and the registry run continuously. The standards themselves take work to maintain, document, and defend.

People have asked how they can support it. This is how.

Support the Institute
You choose the amount. Payment is processed by Stripe on their secure checkout page.

Support does not buy certification

A recording meets the standard or it does not. No payment changes a decision, advances a queue, or creates any standing with the Institute. Certification applies to individual recordings, never to an artist, a label, or a catalog, and that does not change for anyone.

Not a charitable donation

The Musical Form Institute is operated by Muse Foundry LLC, which is not a 501(c)(3) organization. Support is not tax deductible. It is not a purchase, and it carries no goods, services, or benefits in return.
