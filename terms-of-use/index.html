2. About the Site

The Site provides information about the certification programs operated by MFI and hosts a public certification registry (the "Registry") that displays information about sound recordings certified under MFI standards.

MFI administers two certification programs:

  • Certificate of Embodied Production. A process-based certification mark for sound recordings that verifies a specific recording was produced without grid-based timing correction, pitch correction, or generative AI.
  • Certified Significant Form. A certification mark that verifies a sound recording was created without generative AI.

The Registry is a public verification tool. It enables anyone to confirm whether a specific sound recording has been certified under an MFI standard. Registry entries include the certified recording title, artist, certification identifier, certification date, applicable standards version, and related metadata.
